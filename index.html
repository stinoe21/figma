What we do

The world as our office.

We're Caesar, Stijn and Max, three founders who build like everyone our age: heads down, screens on. This summer we do it differently. We walk 120 km of the Camino and build as we go, laptop in the backpack, Starlink overhead, glasses for a screen. The office fits in a bag, and the agents that run our build come with us. Where Figma comes in

We build by voice. Design has to be seen.

On the trail there's no office and no shared screen. The code is written handsfree, by voice and agents, from inside a backpack. But a product isn't only code: it needs a face, a flow, a system someone else can pick up. Figma is where the product stops being words and becomes something we can look at, agree on, and hand over.

The source of truth
Interface, user flows and the design system in one Figma file: the single place the whole product is real and visible, not just described out loud.
Why it fits the walk
We're moving separately, building by voice. Figma is the shared canvas that keeps three people making the same product, with no room to stand in together.
What MIND keeps
Not a frozen build but a living design system: components, flows and tokens MIND's own team can open in Figma and keep developing after we've gone home.

The app we build for MIND

A weather report for your mind.

MIND holds a huge library of help: guides, self-tests, helplines. But you have to find it yourself, and the energy to search is exactly what's missing when things are heavy. So we flip it. One question, how's the weather in your head?, becomes the way in. You answer in weather, and the app hands you one small step from MIND's own content, every day.

Where Figma fits: the whole visual foundation, the weather states, the one-step-a-day flow, the design system, is drawn and documented in Figma. It's the file MIND opens the morning after we finish, and the reason they can keep building.
